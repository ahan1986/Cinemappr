About Cinemapp.r

Going to the movies is all about the shared experience. There's nothing quite like a room full of people laughing, crying or gasping as we all journey through a story together. Cinemapp.r was created with this in mind. We all love going to the movies, so why not create an app that makes going to the movies a much simpler process? Cinemapp.r is your one-stop-shop to find a theater near you with a Google Maps and Google Places integration along with movies playing with their specific showtimes. Below you can find out more about our team and how we each contributed to the Cinemapp.r experience.
